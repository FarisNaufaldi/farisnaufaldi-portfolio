Your concerns, addressed with clarity

What services do you offer?

I specialize in graphic design, video editing, web development, and math education. I create modern, engaging visuals, dynamic video content, functional websites, and clear, effective learning materials tailored to your needs.

Do you provide revisions?

Yes! I offer two free rounds of revisions for design, video, and web projects to ensure the final deliverable perfectly matches your vision. Additional revisions are available at a negotiated rate.

How do I start working with you?

Simply reach out via my contact page or email. We’ll discuss your project goals, scope, and timeline, and I’ll provide a personalized plan and quote.

What is your pricing structure?

Pricing is based on the project’s complexity and duration. Whether it's a video, design, website, or tutoring package, I provide transparent custom quotes to fit your budget.

How long does a project take?

Timelines vary: • Graphic design: 3–7 days • Video editing: 1–2 weeks • Web development: 2–6 weeks • Math tutoring: flexible scheduling Exact timelines will be confirmed during our project discussion.
